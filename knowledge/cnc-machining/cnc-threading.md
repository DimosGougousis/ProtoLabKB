# CNC Threading

*Note: This article could not be fetched from the provided URL. Threading information is available in the mastering-complex-features article.*

## ProtoLabs Threading Capabilities

### Thread Sizes

- **Imperial**: #2-56 up to 1/2-20 (automated factory)
- **Metric**: M1.6x0.35 up to M12x1.75 (automated factory)
- Larger and specialized threads available via Protolabs Network

### Thread Types

- **UNC**: Unified National Coarse
- **UNF**: Unified National Fine
- **Metric**: ISO metric threads

### Thread Modeling Guidelines

- Model holes at nominal diameter (do not model threads)
- Thread depth should not exceed 2x diameter for cost-effectiveness
- Deeper threads available but with increased cost

### Thread Inserts

- Coil inserts (Heli-Coil)
- Key inserts
- Provide longer service life in soft materials
- Available through semi-automated factory or Protolabs Network
