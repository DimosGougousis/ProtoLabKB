# ISO Quality Standards for CNC Machining

## ISO 9001:2015 - Quality Management Systems

### Requirements for CNC Operations
- **Process-Based QMS**: Documented procedures for machining processes
- **Management Review**: Regular assessment of quality system effectiveness
- **Supplier Control**: Qualification and monitoring of material/tool suppliers
- **Traceability**: Lot tracking and records retention (minimum 6 years)
- **Non-Conformance**: Tracking and corrective action for quality issues

### CNC-Specific Applications
- Tool calibration and certification records
- Material certification verification
- Dimensional inspection procedures
- Statistical process control for tolerances
- Equipment maintenance logs

## ISO 13485:2016 - Medical Devices

### Applicability
- CNC machining of medical implants, surgical instruments, diagnostic equipment
- Risk-based approach to design and production
- Biocompatibility and sterility assurance requirements

### Key Requirements
- **Design History File (DHF)**: Complete design rationale and verification
- **Device Master Record (DMR)**: Manufacturing specifications and procedures
- **Risk Management**: ISO 14971 integration for medical device safety
- **Traceability**: Patient-level identification for implants
- **Post-Market Surveillance**: Adverse event reporting and analysis

### Documentation Needs
- Process validation for critical manufacturing steps
- Software validation for CNC programming systems
- Cleanroom and contamination control procedures
- Sterilization validation records

## AS9100D - Aerospace Quality Management

### Beyond ISO 9001 Requirements
- **Foreign Object Debris (FOD)**: Prevention and detection procedures
- **Counterfeit Parts**: Supplier audits and part authenticity verification
- **Configuration Management**: Design and production change control
- **Critical Process Parameters**: Identification and monitoring of safety-critical parameters

### Aerospace-Specific Controls
- Tool life tracking and replacement documentation
- Work area cleanliness verification
- Supplier performance monitoring with contingency plans
- First article inspection requirements

### Documentation Requirements
- Process qualification records
- Tool certification and calibration
- Material traceability to source
- Non-conformance and corrective action reports

## Implementation in CNC Machining
- Quality planning for each machining operation
- Process capability studies (Cp/Cpk)
- Gage R&R studies for measurement systems
- Continuous improvement through data analysis
