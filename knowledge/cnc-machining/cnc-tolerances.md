## Standard ProtoLabs CNC Tolerances

Based on general ProtoLabs machining capabilities:

- **Standard tolerance**: ±0.005" (±0.13mm)
- **Tight tolerance**: ±0.002" (±0.05mm) — available on select features
- **Very tight tolerance**: ±0.001" (±0.025mm) — limited to specific geometries

## Factors Affecting Tolerance

- Part geometry and complexity
- Material selection
- Feature accessibility
- Tool accessibility
- Depth-to-diameter ratios

*Please refer to the ProtoLabs website for the most current tolerance specifications.*
