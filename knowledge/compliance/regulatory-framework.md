## Export Control Regulations

### ITAR (International Traffic in Arms Regulations)

**Registration**: ProtoLabs is ITAR registered (valid through 2026)

**Applicability**:
- Defense articles on the United States Munitions List (USML)
- Technical data related to defense articles
- Defense services

**Requirements**:
- Manufacturing must occur in ITAR-registered facility
- No foreign national access to ITAR-controlled technical data
- End-use certificates may be required
- Secure handling and storage of controlled technical data

**Facility Capabilities**:
- ✅ ITAR-compliant: NC facility (DMLS)
- ✅ ITAR-compliant: MN facilities (CNC Machining, Injection Molding)

### EAR (Export Administration Regulations)

**Classification**: Most commercial parts fall under EAR99 (no license required)

**Export Control Classification Numbers (ECCN)**:
- Standard manufacturing processes: EAR99
- Advanced materials or technologies: May require classification review

**Restricted Parties**:
- Denied Persons List (DPL)
- Entity List
- Specially Designated Nationals (SDN)

### OFAC Sanctions

Compliance with U.S. Treasury Department sanctions programs:
- Country-specific embargoes
- Sectoral sanctions
- Blocked persons and entities

## Data Protection & Cybersecurity

### NIST SP 800-171

**Purpose**: Protect Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) in non-federal systems

**Implementation**:
- Encryption of data at rest and in transit
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Web Application Firewall (WAF)
- File validation and access controls
- Regular penetration testing
- 24/7 security monitoring

**Applicability**: Required for all Department of Defense contracts

### CMMC (Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification)

**Status**: In preparation for certification

**Levels**:
- Level 1: Basic safeguarding of FCI (Federal Contract Information)
- Level 2: Transition to protect CUI (NIST SP 800-171)
- Level 3: Enhanced protection of CUI

**Timeline**: Certification expected within DoD-specified timelines

### GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

**Applicability**: EU customers and data subjects

**Key Requirements**:
- Lawful basis for processing personal data
- Data minimization
- Right to access, rectification, erasure
- Data breach notification (72 hours)
- Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA)

**Facility Compliance**: Germany (Putzbrunn) fully GDPR compliant

### PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)

**Scope**: Payment card data protection

**Compliance**: Annual assessment and attestation

## Industry-Specific Standards

### Aerospace (AS9100 D)

**Scope**: Quality management for aviation, space, and defense

**Key Requirements**:
- Enhanced product traceability
- Configuration management
- Special requirements for critical items
- First Article Inspection (FAI)
- Production Part Approval Process (PPAP)

**Available Processes**:
- ✅ DMLS (NC facility)
- ✅ SLS (NC facility)
- ✅ MJF (NC facility)
- ✅ CNC Machining (MN, NH, UK facilities)

**Additional Capabilities**:
- NADCAP heat treatment (for flight-critical parts)
- Material lot traceability
- Statistical process control (SPC)

### Medical (ISO 13485:2016)

**Scope**: Quality management for medical devices

**Key Requirements**:
- Risk management (ISO 14971)
- Design controls and design history files
- Process validation
- Sterilization validation
- Biocompatibility assessment

**Available Processes**:
- ✅ DMLS (NC facility)
- ✅ Injection Molding (MN facility)

**Material Requirements**:
- Biocompatibility testing (ISO 10993 series)
- USP Class VI (for certain applications)
- FDA material master files

### Automotive (IATF 16949)

**Note**: ProtoLabs Network partners maintain IATF 16949 certification

**Key Requirements**:
- Advanced Product Quality Planning (APQP)
- Production Part Approval Process (PPAP)
- Statistical process control
- Measurement systems analysis (MSA)
- Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA)

## Environmental & Material Regulations

### RoHS (Restriction of Hazardous Substances)

**Scope**: Electrical and electronic equipment

**Restricted Substances**:
- Lead (Pb)
- Mercury (Hg)
- Cadmium (Cd)
- Hexavalent Chromium (Cr6+)
- Polybrominated Biphenyls (PBB)
- Polybrominated Diphenyl Ethers (PBDE)

**Compliance**: Certificates available upon request

### REACH (Registration, Evaluation, Authorization and Restriction of Chemicals)

**Scope**: EU chemicals regulation

**Requirements**:
- Registration of substances
- Safety data sheets (SDS)
- Substance of Very High Concern (SVHC) reporting

**Compliance**: Germany facility REACH compliant

### Conflict Minerals

**Scope**: Tin, tantalum, tungsten, gold (3TG)

**Requirements**:
- Supply chain due diligence
- Conflict Minerals Report (CMR)
- Smelter and refiner identification

**Compliance**: Annual reporting and supply chain assessment

### California Proposition 65

**Scope**: Products sold in California

**Requirements**:
- Warning labels for listed chemicals
- Safe harbor levels compliance

## Compliance Verification Process

### For Regulated Industries

1. **Initial Assessment**
   - Identify applicable regulations
   - Determine required certifications
   - Assess facility capabilities

2. **Documentation Review**
   - Verify material certifications
   - Confirm process qualifications
   - Review quality system documentation

3. **Manufacturing Controls**
   - Route to certified facility
   - Implement special handling procedures
   - Maintain traceability records

4. **Final Verification**
   - Inspection and testing
   - Documentation package review
   - Compliance attestation

### Customer Responsibilities

- Provide clear regulatory requirements
- Supply end-use and end-user information
- Classify export control status (if applicable)
- Specify required documentation
- Verify supplier qualifications
