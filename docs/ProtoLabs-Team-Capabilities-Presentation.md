## 🔧 Cluster 1: Process Agents — Core Manufacturing Expertise

### What This Cluster Drives
**Primary Purpose:** Analyze client part designs against ProtoLabs DFM rules and manufacturing constraints

| Agent | Capability | What It Drives |
|-------|------------|----------------|
| **CNC Machining** | Milled & turned parts, drilling, tapping, threading | Evaluates complex features, tolerances, threading feasibility for aluminum, steel, titanium parts |
| **Injection Molding** | Thermoplastic parts, overmolding, LSR | Validates wall thickness, draft angles, undercuts, gate/runner design, cosmetic requirements |
| **Sheet Metal** | Fabricated parts, bends, flanges, punches | Assesses bend radii, flange clearances, laser cut feasibility, gauge compatibility |
| **3D Printing** | SLA, SLS, MJF, FDM, DMLS, PolyJet | Evaluates layer height, support requirements, orientation, resolution, end-use production readiness |

### Key Value Drivers
- ✅ **Design Validation:** Catches manufacturability issues before quoting
- ✅ **Process Selection:** Recommends best process for part geometry
- ✅ **Cost Optimization:** Identifies features that drive cost/complexity
- ✅ **Quality Assurance:** Ensures designs meet ProtoLabs capability limits

### Compliance Coverage
- ITAR/EAR export controls
- ISO 9001/13485/AS9100 quality standards
- NIST cybersecurity framework
- EU AI Act (for high-risk AI applications)
