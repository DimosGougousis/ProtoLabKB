# AIMS Internal Audit Checklist — ISO/IEC 42001 Clause-by-Clause

> **Purpose:** Detailed audit checklist for conducting ISO/IEC 42001 internal audits of the ProtoLabs AI Management System. Used by internal auditors during Stage 1 and Stage 2 audits.

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-24  
**Owner:** Internal Audit  
**Review Cycle:** Per audit  

---

## Audit Preparation

### Pre-Audit Checklist

- [ ] Audit scope and objectives defined
- [ ] Audit team assigned and competent
- [ ] Audit schedule communicated to auditees
- [ ] Previous audit findings reviewed
- [ ] Relevant documents obtained (charter, policies, procedures)
- [ ] Audit checklist customized for audit focus
- [ ] Opening meeting scheduled
