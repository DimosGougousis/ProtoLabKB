## Evaluation Workflow

| Stage | Description | Gate | Duration | Decision Makers |
|-------|-------------|------|----------|-----------------|
| **1. Ideation** | Opportunity identified from any source | — | Ongoing | All staff |
| **2. Initial Screening** | Quick feasibility and fit assessment | Strategic fit ≥ 3.0 | 1 week | CAIO |
| **3. Detailed Evaluation** | Full business case, risk assessment, resource plan | Risk acceptable, ROI positive | 2-4 weeks | AIMS Steering Committee |
| **4. Approval** | Formal approval to pursue | Board approval if >$X | 1-2 weeks | Board / CAIO |
| **5. Development** | Build, test, validate | Pass all quality gates | Varies | Technical Owner |
| **6. Deployment** | Production release | Certification if required | 2-4 weeks | Safety Officer |
| **7. Review** | Post-deployment value assessment | Value realized | 3 months post-deploy | CAIO |
