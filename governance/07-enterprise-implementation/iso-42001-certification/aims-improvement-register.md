## Improvement Categories

| Category | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Process** | Improvements to AIMS processes | Streamlined risk assessment, faster incident response |
| **Technology** | Improvements to AI systems or infrastructure | Model architecture upgrades, monitoring enhancements |
| **Competence** | Improvements to team capabilities | Training programs, hiring, knowledge sharing |

## Priority Matrix

| Priority | Criteria | Approval Authority |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| **Critical** | Safety impact, certification risk, legal compliance | AIMS Owner + Board |
| **High** | Significant performance gain, major client benefit, substantial risk reduction | AIMS Owner |
| **Medium** | Moderate improvement, operational efficiency, minor risk reduction | Policy Owner |
| **Low** | Nice-to-have, minimal impact | Technical Owner |

## Status Definitions

| Status | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Proposed** | Improvement identified, awaiting evaluation |
| **Approved** | Evaluated and approved for implementation |
| **In Progress** | Implementation actively underway |
| **Implemented** | Improvement deployed and operational |
| **Rejected** | Evaluated and determined not viable |
| **Deferred** | Approved but postponed to future period |
