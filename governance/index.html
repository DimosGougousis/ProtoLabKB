The problem we solve

Every stakeholder has a governance gap.

AI agents are proliferating across your organization. Without governance, every agent is a regulatory, financial, and reputational risk. Here is how the platform closes the gap for each role.

⚖

Board / C-Suite

Tier 1 Board Dashboard

No visibility into the AI agent fleet. Cannot answer “how many agents do we have?” or “are they compliant?” Audit preparation takes weeks of manual evidence gathering.

Platform Solution

60-second executive overview: fleet size, SAFEST compliance %, audit readiness score, ARI distribution, and containment status — all in a single dashboard.

Business Outcome

Audit cost reduction. Automated Article 11 documentation replaces manual evidence gathering. Regulatory transparency for supervisors (DNB, AFM). Board-level accountability without operational burden.
