Executive

Board-level summaries, dashboards, and reporting frameworks for executive visibility into AI governance. Provides strategic oversight and accountability at the highest level.

When to Use

Executive governance materials are used for:

  • Board meetings and quarterly reviews
  • Regulatory examinations (DNB, ECB)
  • Investor due diligence
  • Strategic planning and risk assessment
  • AI Ethics Board oversight

Frequency: Quarterly reports with real-time dashboard access for continuous visibility.

Key Metrics

Executive visibility includes:

  1. Portfolio health — risk distribution across all AI systems
  2. Compliance posture — regulatory adherence by framework
  3. Governance maturity — adoption metrics across pillars
  4. Incident summary — critical events and response effectiveness
  5. Audit readiness — preparation status for regulatory examinations

Regulatory Alignment

  • EU AI Act Article 9 — Risk management system at organizational level
  • DORA Article 5 — Governance and management of ICT risk
  • DNB Good Practice for AI — Board accountability for AI risk
  • ECB Guidance — Supervisory expectations for AI in banking
