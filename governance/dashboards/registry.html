What This Delivers

Definitive answers to regulatory inventory questions

The first question regulators ask: "How many AI agents do you operate and what are their risk classifications?" This registry provides the definitive answer with searchable inventory, EU AI Act risk tiers, and compliance tracking.

📋

EU AI Act Risk Classification

Every agent categorized by Minimal, Limited, High, or Unacceptable risk tier for instant regulatory reporting.

✓

Contract Compliance Matrix

Track all 7 SAFEST contract statuses per agent—identify gaps and prioritize remediation at a glance.

🔍

Multi-Factor Search & Filter

Find any agent instantly by risk tier, team, oversight model, or compliance status. No more manual spreadsheets.

🌐

Dutch Algorithm Register Export

One-click export formatted for DNB/AFM supervisor submission. IAMA compliance built-in.

When you need this

Use Cases

📋 Regulatory Inventory Report

Generate a complete agent inventory categorized by EU AI Act risk tier: Minimal, Limited, High, or Unacceptable. Ready for supervisor submission.

🔍 Contract Compliance Audit

Filter agents with incomplete contract compliance (< 7/7) to prioritize remediation efforts. Focus on the gaps that matter most.

🆕 New Agent Onboarding

Register a new agent with identity (C1), assign risk tier, set oversight model, and track onboarding progress through the registry.

🏷️ Version Tracking

Trace which agent versions are in production, staged, or deprecated. Maintain version control across your entire fleet.

🌐 Cross-Team Inventory

Organization-wide view for the CAIO: which teams run which agents at what risk level? Answer in seconds, not days.
