Team Dashboard

Live operational control of your team's agent fleet.

Per-agent visibility scoped to your team only. Monitor health, cost, drift, and performance—all in real time.

Primary: Team Leads / Product Managers
What This Delivers

Live operational control for your team's agent fleet

Team leads need per-agent visibility: which agents are healthy, which are drifting, and which are consuming excessive resources. This dashboard is scoped to your team only, preventing information overload while surfacing the metrics that matter for day-to-day operations.

💚

Real-Time Agent Health

Instant status on all team agents—Healthy, Degraded, or Critical. Perfect for daily standups and quick triage.

💰

Per-Agent Cost Tracking

C4 contract monitoring prevents runaway spend before it hits finance. Spot anomalies like 340% cost spikes instantly.

📉

Drift Detection & Alerts

C5 contract monitoring catches model degradation before customers notice. Protect revenue and user experience proactively.

⚡

Live KPIs vs. Monthly Reports

Replace manual spreadsheet compilation with real-time data. Get answers in seconds, not days.

When you need this

Use Cases

🌅 Daily Standup Review

Glance at the agent health table during standup: which agents are healthy, degraded, or under containment? Assign follow-ups before the meeting ends.

💰 Cost Anomaly Detection

Agent X cost jumped 340% this week. The dashboard flags it—now investigate, contain, or scale down before the monthly bill arrives.

📉 Drift Alert Response

C5 contract flags performance drift on your recommendation agent. Review the trend, compare to baseline, and decide on containment level.

📊 Cross-Agent Comparison

Compare ARI scores across your team's agent fleet. Identify which agents need attention and which are performing optimally.

📈 Capacity Planning

Use health trends and containment history to plan agent scaling. Know when your infrastructure needs expansion before performance degrades.
